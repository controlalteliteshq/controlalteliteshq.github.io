Who Are We?

We are a student-led club, built by students for students. We aim to create a community of learners, builders, and leaders.

Our Partner

We proudly partner with Hack Club, a USA-registered non-profit (501(c)(3)).

What You'll Learn

  • Hardware projects and electronics
  • Creative tools and design using Figma Pro
  • Presentation, communication, and public speaking

  • Free access to Figma Pro and other pro tools

  • Hands-on project and hardware experience

  • Grants and funding opportunities via Hack Club
  • Merit-based leadership roles within the club
