Who Are We?

We are a student-led STEM club, built by students for students. We aim to create a community of learners, builders, and leaders who are passionate about science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Every day, our members gather to explore STEM fields through hands-on projects and experiments, whether you're a beginner or have years of experience, there's a place for you at Control Alt Elite.

Our Partner

We proudly partner with Hack Club, a USA-registered non-profit (501(c)(3)) dedicated to empowering the next generation of makers. Together, we're building a global community of student hackers.

What You'll Learn

  • Scientific principles and experimental methods
  • Programming and computational thinking
  • Engineering design and problem-solving
  • Mathematical concepts and their real-world applications
  • Presentation, communication, and public speaking skills
  • Collaboration and teamwork in a supportive environment

Benefits of Joining

  • Access to professional STEM tools and resources
  • Certificates of participation for your portfolio
  • Hands-on project and laboratory experience
  • Science fairs, competitions, and workshop opportunities
  • Grants and funding opportunities for STEM projects
  • Merit-based leadership roles within the club
  • Connect with other STEM enthusiasts worldwide
