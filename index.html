Who Are We?

We are a student-led club, built by students for students. We aim to create a community of learners, builders, and leaders who are passionate about technology and innovation.

Every day, our members gather to make things with code, whether you're a beginner programmer or have years of experience, there's a place for you at Control Alt Elite.

Our Partner

We proudly partner with Hack Club, a USA-registered non-profit (501(c)(3)) dedicated to empowering the next generation of makers. Together, we're building a global community of student hackers.

What You'll Learn

  • Software development and coding through hands-on projects
  • Hardware projects and electronics with real-world applications
  • Creative tools and design using Figma Pro
  • Presentation, communication, and public speaking skills
  • Collaboration and teamwork in a supportive environment

Benefits of Joining

  • Free access to Figma Pro and other professional tools
  • Certificates of participation for your portfolio
  • Hands-on project and hardware experience
  • Webinars, hackathons, and workshop opportunities
  • Grants and funding opportunities via Hack Club
  • Merit-based leadership roles within the club
  • Connect with other student hackers worldwide
